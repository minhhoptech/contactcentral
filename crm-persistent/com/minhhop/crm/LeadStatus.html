[Groovy] Class LeadStatus

      • com.minhhop.crm.LeadStatus

      • Use the lead status field.

        Equally important to lead sources are lead statuses: you need a picklist of lead statuses that mirrors your engagement process.

        Depending on your business process, you probably want some options like No contact, Email intro, Meeting request, Future, and Closed

        Default Lead Status: Unqualified, New, Working, Nurturing, Qualified
