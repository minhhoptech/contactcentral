[Groovy] Class Opportunity

      • com.minhhop.crm.Opportunity

      • A opportunity contains the information for every deal you’re tracking, such as deal size and expected close date. The opportunity object is at the core of your sales process. By managing it correctly, you’ll get the most value from your investment in CRM.
